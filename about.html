About ShipDecKK

Learn about our history, mission, and values

Our Story

Founded in 2020, ShipDecKK began with a simple vision: to revolutionize the maritime industry by offering exceptional ships and unparalleled customer service. What started as a small dealership specializing in luxury yachts has quickly grown into a comprehensive maritime solutions provider serving clients worldwide.

Our founder, Captain Katherine Kimball, combined her extensive naval experience with a passion for ship design to create a company that truly understands the needs of maritime clients, from individual yacht owners to commercial shipping enterprises.

Today, ShipDecKK stands as a testament to that original vision, having expanded our fleet and services while maintaining our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional maritime solutions through quality vessels, expert service, and a customer-first approach, empowering our clients to achieve their maritime goals with confidence and satisfaction.
